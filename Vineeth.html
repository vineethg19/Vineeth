      Do you Know ?

  1) During World War II, Northwest Missouri State University
  was one of 131 colleges and universities nationally that took part
  in the V-12 Navy College Training Program which offered students
  a path to a Navy commission.

  2) On August 14, 1972, Northwest was elevated to university
  status so that it could offer master's degrees.Its name changed
  to Northwest Missouri State University.
